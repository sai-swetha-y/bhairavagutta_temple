Temple Distinction

Guru sannidhi, sacred hill, devotional stillness

  • Sri Maha Kala Bhairava Swamy is revered here as Swayambhu.
  • Abhaya Shaneeswara Swamy stands in the grace of his Guru.
  • The shrine rests inside a spiritually vibrant mini forest.
Ancient Presence Bhairava Gutta has been locally revered for more than 300 years.
Sacred Installation 6-foot Sri Abhaya Shaneeswara deity installed on 27 February 2003.
Rare Significance First Tailabhishekam was performed on 1 March 2003 on a rare sacred combination day.
Living Green Shrine More than 10,000 sacred and medicinal plants nurture the temple land.
